Vehicle update

● Preparations of the car keep going at full throttle!

This past 30th of April, the chassis and motor departments have met in the workshop to carry out several checks and define the next tasks to be completed.

The chassis department has carried out a review of the CAD of the vehicle and the tolerances of all the chassis parts available. In addition to checking that all of them follow the regulations of the Formula Student competition.

The motor department has welcomed 4 new members who participated in the leak test of the gasoline tank of the vehicle. It was decided that there is a need to improve the tank to ensure that it is completely sealed for competition. In addition, the support bearings that will be used for the test bench have been chosen.

Likewise, during this last week, the marketing and communications department, with the help of other members of the team, have carried out activities within the framework of the UdG Festival with the aim of opening up to the student body. A very successful activity that has managed to attract the attention of several interested parties who have come to ask for information about the project.

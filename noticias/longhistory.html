Our team proudly continues the legacy of the original UdG Racing Team, a student-led initiative from the University of Girona (Universitat de Girona). The team made its debut in the Formula Student Spain competition in 2016, showcasing the talent and dedication of engineering students passionate about motorsport and innovation. Over the years, the team grew with the support of sponsors like INS Montilivi, RS Components, and Caixa d’Enginyers.
Today, we build on the foundation they established—carrying forward their spirit, evolving with new technologies, and aiming to achieve new milestones in student engineering competitions.

It was a Thursday, and the whole team had set aside the day to meet at the Laboratory of Motors at UdG. The goal was clear: clean and prepare the workshop for the work ahead.
From the morning onwards, everyone got involved—sweeping, organizing tools, clearing old parts, and setting up workspaces. The place had been unused for a while, but little by little, it started to feel like home.
By the end of the day, the workshop was clean, organized, and ready for a fresh start. More than just a tidy space, it marked the beginning of our journey as a team—ready to build, learn, and move forward together.
